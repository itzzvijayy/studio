# **App Name**: Vision-AI Clean Madurai

## Core Features:

- Waste Complaint Submission: Users can upload images of waste along with their geographic location to report environmental concerns in Madurai.
- AI Waste Detection & Analysis: An AI tool automatically processes uploaded images to detect the presence of waste, classify its type, and determine its severity.
- Complaint Tracking & Status Updates: Users can view and track the status and details of their submitted waste complaints, including images, locations, and AI analysis results.
- User Profiles: Users can create and manage their personal profiles, where their submitted complaints and related activities are recorded.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme will be used to convey a sense of openness and natural vibrancy.
- Primary color: A deep forest green (#1B5E20), symbolizing nature, growth, and ecological balance, providing a grounding yet impactful presence against the light background.
- Background color: A very light, almost white gray (#F5F5F5) will serve as the primary background, offering a clean, airy canvas for content.
- Accent color: A strong, clear civic blue (#0D47A1) will be used to highlight key interactive elements and reinforce themes of community, trust, and responsibility.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly legible appearance across all text sizes. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Utilize simple, outline-style icons with an eco-friendly and community-oriented theme to maintain a clean visual identity.
- A clean and intuitive layout inspired by Material Design 3 principles, prioritizing clear information hierarchy and user-friendly navigation.
- Subtle, smooth transitions and micro-interactions for map movements, content loading, and feedback on user actions to enhance the user experience.
